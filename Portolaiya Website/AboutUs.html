- About Us -

Porto Laiya is a 35-hectare coastal retreat in San Juan Batangas, offering exclusive residential lots along pristine white sands.

This private beach resort community promises a tranquil escape from city life. With a gated beach enclave, a private beach club, and a 400-meter beachfront, it's perfect for sea lovers. Enjoy stunning mountain views and explore the protected reef for swimming and snorkeling.

Active Group Inc., a real estate developer in the Philippines, has been a pioneering force for over five decades. From social housing projects to urban skyscrapers and premier leisure havens, the company has shaped the Philippine landscape with a diverse range of impactful developments.

Originally founded in 1970 as architectural firm Turalba and Associates, Active Group has evolved into a multibillion-peso real estate powerhouse delivering end-to-end expertise, providing Filipinos consistent quality developments and innovative solutions.
